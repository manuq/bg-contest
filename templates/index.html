Sugarlabs Background Image Contest

Sugarlabs is glad to announce the first contest for Sugar Background Images. We'll choose 10 images that will form a pack of user-contributed backgrounds.

1 - Create your background image!

  • Be creative! You can use Paint or Turtle Blocks, Record to take photos, or any other Sugar activity.
  • You are not restricted to Sugar activities.
  • You can submit as many entries as you like.
  • Try to use size 1600 x 1200 pixels. Or if you are using a Sugar activity, use its default size.

2 - Test it in Sugar

  • Place your background in Documents.
  • Go to My Settings -> Background.
  • Select your background.

3 - Apply to these terms and conditions:

  • You must be the author of the image, derivative work is allowed.
  • You must use the CC-by-sa license.
  • All data collected will be used for the Sugar Labs Background Image contest only. It will not be used for any other purposes.
  • Please use PNG format.
