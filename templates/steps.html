How to test your background images

If you are using Sugar 0.100 or later, you can test your background image following these steps.

1 - Use any activity you want.

2 - Be creative!

3 - Copy your image to the Documents folder.

4 - Go to "My Settings" and then "Background" to select your image.

5 - It's ready!
